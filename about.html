About Me!

My name is Michael Van Orman, and I've been studying art since high school. While I didn't draw or make much art when I was younger, I found my love for art in a 9th grade digital art class. After that, I eventually got into the Chesterfield County Tech Center in my senior year, and would get into Brightpoint community college where I currently study.

I hope that in the future I can display my skills for many to see, whether that be via graphic design to gallery work.

In my spare time, I like to play video games and journal. I've been journaling for about 2.5 years now, and I would reccomend it to anyone and everyone! It has really helped me appreciate the little things more.
